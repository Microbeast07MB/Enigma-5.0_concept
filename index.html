Event Flow

A clear path from idea to demo.

  1. Step 1 Register Your Team

    Form a team of 4 undergraduate students and submit your details.

  2. Step 2 Pick a Track

    Select FinTech or HealthTech based on the problem your team wants to solve.

  3. Step 3 Build and Mentor

    Prototype your solution, refine the user flow, and prepare your final pitch.

  4. Step 4 Demo and Win

    Present to judges and compete for the ₹25,000 prize pool.
